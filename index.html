About

I have spent the past three years being a Jack of All Trades in the video game and radio industry.  I can do a variety of things.  I design and manage sites, create graphics, film and edit video, 3D model, manage social media and brainstorm new solutions with teams.

In the gaming industry, I worked in VR, developed for Epic Games, the Kennedy Space Center, supported Indiebox, Indiecade and other local Orlando startups.  In broadcasting, I developed the sites, social media, digital graphics, print graphics and newsletters for a cluster of seven very different stations.
