About

I am a highly motivated multi-media director with over three years of experience across multiple industries, including radio, freelance graphic design, freelance web design and video games. Major tasks include: website design, filming and video editing, 3D modeling, digital graphics, social media management and market research and strategy building.

My work in the gaming industry involved developing for virtual reality, multiple platforms including mobile and PC, developing for the Kennedy Space Center and Epic Games, managing a successful Kickstarter campaign and supporting Orlando start-up companies such as Indiebox and IndieCade.

As a media director in broadcasting, I completed a variety of tasks for major radio stations iHeartRadio and Beasley Media. Specific duties included: website development, podcast production, guest starring onair, managing social media, digital and print graphics for events, newsletters and market research and strategy building.
